Top Tourist Places in Surabaya

Heroes Monument (Tugu Pahlawan)

The Heroes Monument is the most iconic landmark in Surabaya, commemorating the fallen heroes of the Battle of Surabaya during Indonesia’s independence war. The towering structure stands as a symbol of the city’s courage and patriotism. The monument is located within a park, accompanied by a museum that provides historical context about the battle and the city’s role in Indonesia’s independence.

Suramadu Bridge

The Suramadu Bridge is the longest bridge in Indonesia, connecting the city of Surabaya with Madura Island. Spanning 5.4 kilometers across the Madura Strait, the bridge offers stunning views of the surrounding waters. It is not just an engineering marvel but also a major gateway to explore the beauty of both Surabaya and Madura.

Kenjeran Beach

Kenjeran Beach is a popular coastal destination in Surabaya. It's perfect for enjoying the sunrise, taking a relaxing stroll, or visiting the nearby Sanggar Agung Temple, a stunning Chinese temple that sits right on the coastline. The area is also known for its seafood restaurants and recreational spots, making it an ideal place for both relaxation and cultural exploration.
