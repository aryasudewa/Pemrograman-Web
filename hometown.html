Welcome to Surabaya

Surabaya, the capital city of East Java, is one of Indonesia's largest and most vibrant cities. Known as the "City of Heroes" due to its significant role in Indonesiaâ€™s independence, Surabaya is a bustling metropolis that blends rich history with modernity.

The city offers a mix of cultural heritage and rapid urban development. From the majestic Heroes Monument, honoring the battle for Indonesia's freedom, to the thriving business districts, Surabaya is a symbol of resilience and progress.

Beyond the historical sites, Surabaya boasts a lively culinary scene, beautiful parks, and is a major gateway to iconic destinations such as Mount Bromo and the Madura Island via the stunning Suramadu Bridge.

Despite its size, Surabaya retains a sense of warmth and community, making it not just a city of commerce, but a welcoming home for those who visit and live here.
